Grammar: Happening Now

How does Jay describe what he is doing in the video?

"I am standing in the lobby."

"I am walking down the corridor."

Select the correct rule:

To describe an action happening exactly RIGHT NOW, we use:

RULE UNLOCKED!

This is the Present Continuous tense. Use it to narrate your life!

What are you doing right now? (e.g. "I am sitting", "I am learning").
